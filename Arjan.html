WHO WE ARE

Source Area Solutions, founded in 2022, is a premier provider of IT solutions and systems integration services, specializing in Managed Network and Infrastructure Services, Web Hosting and Development, and comprehensive Technology Consulting. With a strong emphasis on business technology, the company is committed to helping organizations build future-ready enterprises.

By delivering tailored solutions and strategic expertise, SAS enables businesses to navigate the complexities of the digital landscape, fostering innovation, efficiency, and sustainable growth. Guided by its tagline, “Implementing the Right Technological Solutions”, the company remains dedicated to delivering impactful results and driving transformative change across industries.

MISSION

Our mission is to deliver and implement the right technological solutions, empowering our clients to focus on their core business while we expertly handle the complexities of the digital world.

VISION

Our vision is to be the go-to IT services provider, setting the standard for innovative solutions and unmatched after-sales support.

Arjan Biscarra

Chief Technology Officer

Arjan brings over a decade of experience in IT systems integration, with a proven track record across the telecommunications, healthcare, and web hosting industries. As CTO of Source Area Solutions, he leads the company’s technology strategy—blending deep technical expertise with hands-on leadership.

Arjan specializes in IT infrastructure, cloud computing, cybersecurity, and network integration. He holds certifications from Cisco, Sophos, and Peplink, and has held key engineering roles at SpeedFusion Networks, Meshnetworks, and Handlink Inc.

Before establishing SAS, he served as IT Manager at ARC Hospitals, where he built the hospital’s entire IT system from the ground up—a career-defining project.

Arjan holds a degree in Computer Engineering from the University of Cebu – Lapu-Lapu and Mandaue. His passion lies in delivering secure, scalable solutions that move businesses forward.

Alexis Mahusay

Technical Engineer

Alexis brings extensive experience in structured cabling, with a strong background in designing and installing reliable, future-ready network infrastructure. Skilled in both copper and fiber optic systems, Alexis delivers scalable, organized cabling solutions that support critical data, voice, and video needs.

From fast-paced commercial builds to complex system upgrades, Alexis combines technical precision with a commitment to clean, reliable execution—always aiming to exceed client expectations. Behind every project is a dedication to quality, efficiency, and the belief that a well-built network is the foundation of every successful operation.

“My journey into structured cabling started with a screwdriver and a curiosity for how things connect. Over the years, that curiosity turned into a career designing and installing networks that power businesses.”

Sparks Fabros

Sales & Operations Manager

Sparks is a seasoned Sales & Operations Manager with a proven track record in driving growth, streamlining operations, and delivering exceptional customer value in the Information Technology industry. With almost two decades of experience, he combines strategic thinking with hands-on leadership to build scalable systems and high-performing teams.

He holds a Bachelor of Science in Computer Engineering degree from Cebu Institute of Technology – University and has held key positions in respected tech companies, including Pacific Internet Philippines, SpeedFusion Networks, Handlink Inc., and FatPipe Networks, Inc.

His passion for technology, paired with his dedication to operational excellence, continues to drive innovation and measurable results for the clients and for the teams he leads.

He began his career as a Sales Associate and quickly rose through the ranks to become a Sales Manager—refining his leadership skills and sharpening his expertise in client engagement and strategic sales execution.

Known for his deep understanding of customer needs and market dynamics, Dee Mark has built strong relationships with clients and partners alike. His strategic, people-centered approach consistently uncovers new growth opportunities, fosters trust, and drives the successful development of key accounts.

Dee Mark holds a degree in Commerce and remains deeply committed to nurturing long-term, value-driven partnerships that contribute to the sustainable growth of SAS and its affiliates.

Abegail brings a strategic and client-focused approach to managing accounts and driving sales performance. With a solid background in account management, she specializes in identifying customer needs, delivering tailored solutions, and consistently exceeding client expectations.

Abegail is known for her proactive coordination with both clients and internal teams to ensure seamless execution and service delivery. Her strategic mindset allows her to uncover new business opportunities, strengthen client loyalty, and fuel account growth.

Account Manager

With her warm, approachable demeanor and keen listening skills, she effortlessly puts clients at ease and crafts solutions that not only meet — but exceed — their expectations.

Aulene Alandroque

Finance & HR Head

Aulene leads the Finance and Human Resources departments at Source Area Solutions, bringing over two decades of experience in financial planning, analysis, and reporting. She is known for her strong analytical acumen and technical expertise in conducting audit testing, identifying control gaps, safeguarding assets, and developing actionable recommendations to enhance operational efficiency and compliance.

Before joining SAS, Aulene served as a Tax Compliance Officer at Weld Powertools and Construction Corporation, where she ensured strict adherence to tax laws and regulations. Her role included reviewing financial records, preparing tax filings, and advising on tax-related matters to minimize liabilities and ensure accurate reporting. Her responsibilities span from foundational compliance tasks to the development of tax strategies and team leadership.

Aulene holds a Bachelor of Science in Accountancy from Cebu Institute of Technology-University and a Bachelor of Science in Management Accounting from the University of San Jose-Recoletos. She remains dedicated to improving business processes, enhancing financial reporting, and driving compliance—contributing to the continued growth and financial success of Source Area Solutions.
